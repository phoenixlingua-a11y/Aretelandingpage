How it works

Four layers. One signal.
Calibrated to you, not to a population.

Areté is built around the principle that no single biomarker is sufficient to read cognitive integrity. The signal lives in the convergence — across autonomic state, behavioral output, and contextual load — measured against the operator's own onboarding baseline.

01 / Calibration · v3
Forensic Spectrum
A non-verbal calibration session maps the operator's cognitive function profile across two processing axes. This becomes the personal baseline — the standard against which every future ambient reading is measured. Working in v3 →
02 / Autonomic · v3
Body Signal
Heart rate variability, sleep architecture, and thermal baseline are sampled on-device through HealthKit. Raw values stay on the device. Areté receives derived signal scores only — privacy by architecture, not by policy. Working in v3 →
03 / Behavioral · Roadmap
Behavioral Signal
Vocal pitch variation, typing cadence, micro-tremor, and gaze patterns are fused with the autonomic layer. Behavioral signals reveal cognitive load before the body does. The convergence between body and behavior is where the strongest predictive signal lives. Roadmap · 2026–27 →
04 / Output · v3
Intercept Directive
When drift exceeds threshold, Areté surfaces a directive — not a metric. A wrist haptic in the moment, a pre-meeting briefing on the phone, or a contextual warning before an irreversible action. One signal, three delivery vectors, no dashboard to interpret. Working in v3 →

Every meeting, every commit, every irreversible call enters the ledger with its concurrent biometric signature. Thirty days later, the outcome is scored. Sixty days later, the score is refined. The operator does not have to interpret. The ledger does the work in the background and surfaces only the divergence: the moments when your signal said one thing and your decision said another.
